The exercises are chosen from the textbook: Hildebrand, Francis. Advanced Calculus for Applications. 2nd ed. Englewood Cliffs: Prentice Hall, March 31, 1976. ISBN: 0130111899. Homeworks will neither be graded nor collected, and will not count towards your final grade. There is no homework in the week of lectures 13 and 14.
